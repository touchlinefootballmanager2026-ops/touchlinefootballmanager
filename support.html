Common questions

Where are my saves stored?
Saves are kept locally on your device. The game has no account and no cloud sync, so your career lives on the device it was created on. Deleting the app removes its saves.

Does the game work offline?
Yes. Touchline Football Manager is fully playable offline. The only things that use the network are optional anonymous analytics (if you consent) and processing an in-app purchase.

What are the in-app purchases?
Optional one-time "Budget Boost" and "Major Investment" items that add in-game funds to your transfer budget. Everything in the game is fully playable without buying anything, and they confer no real-world value.

How do I turn analytics off?
Go to Settings → Usage Analytics and turn it off. When off, nothing is sent and any locally stored events are cleared.

My in-app purchase didn't deliver — what do I do?
First, try reopening the game. If your budget still hasn't updated, email us with the approximate date and time and which item you bought, and we'll help.

How do I report a bug?
Email the address above with your device model, your OS version, and a description of what happened (and what you expected). Screenshots help.
